Brown Dwarfs

A brown dwarf
Brown dwarfs are stellar objects that have a mass smaller than the lightest stars and larger than the heaviest gas giants. The mass is too low for nuclear reactions of hydrogen in its center to start. However, they are believed to be able to fuse deuterium and burn lithium, emitting faint light in the visible wavelengths
