Main Sequence Stars

Main sequence stars are the most common of all stars in the universe, these stars are also called "dwarfs" and come in many different colors. They can be blue, red, yellow, orange or white.

Giants

Giant class stars are much bigger variants of the normal main sequence stars. These giants come in diffrent classes, from subgiants to hypergiants, these stars also come in a variety colors.

White dwarfs

White dwarfs are the remnants from dead main sequence stars. They are tiny compared to normal sized stars only reaching a couple thousand kilometers i diamiter.

Neutron Stars

Neutron stars are one of the most extreme places in the universe. With only an average diameter of 20 kilomiters these stars can still have the same mass as our sun.

Anomalies

Some stars does not fit in to any catogories but still deserves to be mentioned. These stars include brown dwarfs, black dwarfs and black holes.
